The Genesis



DaneBeats Entertainment is a company established by Dane Saunders and his Business partner Akhona Mqoboli in the pursuit of creativity, individuality and quality in the entertainment industry.


The Music



Danebeats has been involved in numerous musical projects, including the likes of Chu Da Chum, Maluti Langa, Sim Gee, Tyson Sean, Trillae Chad etc.


The Style




Music production from Danebeats Entertainment takes a position of "Urban Kasi Rap" with a touch of classic rap & Hip-hop, Dane has the vision of campturing the old school kwaito-style while blending it with urban rap & culture, look out for our production


The Dream



DaneBeats entertainment aims at being a role player and paving it's own lane but also playing an active role in creating a society that serves justice and deals with the injustices of the system, we are a humanitarian company.




About Dane Beats Entertainment

Danebeats Entertainment is a company and recording label looking to provide a unique sound for people to relate to and enjoy. The record label focuses on the true artistry of music and bringing out the best in each artist and expanding the label into different businesses.
Dane beats is a record label focussed on bringing out the true artistry of each artist and the uniqueness of sound to the world.




The Creator


Dane Saunders, an ambitious, passionate Sound inventer and Sound Designer with vast and outstreched years of experience, had a vision of creating a sound and style that people could relate and jam to, with the establishment of his true skills, he took it upon himself to create a legacy for himself and the one's around him, hence the birth of DaneBeats Entertainment a ground-breaking recording studio for the true artistic artists in South Africa.
